WHAT IS PARK?

The PARK system allows you to record yourself doing voice and motor tasks from the United Parkinson's Disease Rating Scale. The recorded tasks will be collected and analyzed to detect symptoms of Parkinson's Disease.

By recording, you consent to share your videos for research purposes.

Click here to download Google Chrome.

(You must use Google Chrome to visit this site.)

This research is being conducted by the ROC HCI Lab at the University of Rochester in conjunction with the Center for Human Experimental Therapeutics at the University of Rochester Medical Center.
